Our Story

Shanghai Sangamam was born from a simple feeling — the longing for home. Tamil-speaking Indians have been calling Shanghai home for years, building careers, raising families, and carrying their culture thousands of miles from their roots.

We came together to make sure no Tamil in Shanghai ever feels alone. Whether you just landed or you've been here for years, this is your community. Vanakkam! 🙏

From Pongal celebrations to New Year get-togethers, from WhatsApp groups to community dinners — Shanghai Sangamam is where Tamil hearts connect across China. 🌏❤️
