Our Story

Born from community, built with love — Shanghai Sangamam has been connecting Tamil hearts across China since 2004.

Who We Are

Shanghai Sangamam was born from a simple feeling — the longing for home. Tamil-speaking Indians have been calling Shanghai home for years, building careers, raising families, and carrying their culture thousands of miles from their roots.

Since 2004, we have been the gathering point for Tamil hearts in China. Whether you just landed or you've been here for two decades — this is your community. Vanakkam! 🙏

From Pongal celebrations to New Year get-togethers, from WhatsApp groups to community dinners — Shanghai Sangamam is where Tamil families connect, support each other, and celebrate the culture that defines us. 🌏❤️
